Welcome to page 2, on this page you can look at computing history :D Computing history spans from ancient manual tools like the abacus to modern, ubiquitous AI-driven systems. Key milestones include Charles Babbage’s 19th-century mechanical "Analytical Engine" (with programmer Ada Lovelace), the 1940s development of electronic, programmable computers like ENIAC and Turing machines, and the transition from vacuum tubes to transistors, microprocessors, and personal computers.
